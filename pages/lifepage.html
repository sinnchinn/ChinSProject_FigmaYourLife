Nana Osaki was born in Meguro, Tokyo. Her mother left her in the care of her grandmother and she hasn't seen her since. Her grandmother, Miyuki Osaki, was very strict. She forbade her from wearing anything pink or red because she was afraid that she would tempt men like her mother did. After her grandmother passed away, she was left to live on her own.

Growing up, Nana was rebellious and intimidating to others. She was expelled from school by being falsely accused of prostitution, which she didn't fight the charges for. Nana didn't have any friends in school. This was until she befriended Nobu Terashima who lent her CD's, introducing her to punk bands. Nobu takes Nana to a punk concert where she meets her soon to be fiance, Ren Honjo. She was captivated by him. That same night, Ren invites Nana to join a band that he, Nobu and another friend Yasushi Takagi are forming. One of her dreams is to become a lead singer. She accepts the offer. Later down the story, she meets another friend, Shinichi Okazaki , someone who ends up replacing Ren from their band. Another very important friend she meets has the same name as her, Nana Komatsu, which she nicknames Hachi.
